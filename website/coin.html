Features

Superior Technology Stack

01

ERC-20 + ERC-2612 Standard

Full ERC-20 compliance with ERC-2612 Permit extension for gasless approvals. Compatible with every wallet, DEX, and DeFi protocol.

02

Configurable Fee System

Default 8 basis points (0.08%) ‚Äî adjustable up to 20 bps max. Fee exemptions available per address. All fees auto-collected to the reserve wallet.

03

Mint & Burn with Safety Caps

Per-transaction and daily minting caps prevent unauthorized inflation. Controlled supply management with full on-chain audit trail.

04

Compliance & Emergency Controls

Address blacklisting for regulatory compliance, emergency pause/unpause for the entire token, and reentrancy protection on all critical functions.

05

Multi-Send Batch Transfers

Send to up to 200 recipients in a single transaction. Ideal for payroll, airdrops, and distributions ‚Äî saving 90%+ in gas costs.
