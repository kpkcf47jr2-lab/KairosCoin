Privacy Policy

Kairos 777 Inc.

Effective Date: February 27, 2026 · Last Updated: February 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kairos 777 Inc. ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our products and services, including Kairos Trade, Kairos Coin (KAIROS), and Kairos Wallet (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Wallet Address: When you connect your cryptocurrency wallet to our Services, we receive your public wallet address. We do not collect or store your private keys.
  • Transaction Data: When you buy, sell, transfer, or redeem KAIROS tokens, the transaction details are recorded on the public blockchain.
  • Contact Information: If you reach out to us via email or support channels, we may collect your email address and message contents.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymized analytics about how you interact with our websites and applications, including pages visited, features used, and session duration.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type, and screen resolution.
  • IP Address: Collected for security, fraud prevention, and compliance purposes.

1.3 Blockchain Data

All KAIROS token transactions are recorded on public blockchains (BNB Smart Chain, Base, Arbitrum, Polygon). This data is publicly accessible and not controlled by us.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve our Services
  • To process transactions and maintain on-chain records
  • To communicate with you regarding updates, support, or security alerts
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and ensure compliance with applicable laws
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Blockchain Networks: Transaction data is inherently public on blockchain networks.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party providers (hosting, analytics) who assist us in operating our Services, bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Security: To protect the rights, property, and safety of Kairos 777, our users, and the public.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, access controls, and secure infrastructure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

  • Backend data is hosted on secure, encrypted servers
  • We do not store private keys, seed phrases, or wallet passwords
  • API communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (note: blockchain transactions cannot be deleted)
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@kairos-777.com.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our websites may use minimal cookies for essential functionality (e.g., session management). We do not use third-party advertising cookies. Analytics data is collected anonymously.

7. Third-Party Services

Our Services may integrate with third-party platforms including:

  • Blockchain networks (BNB Smart Chain, Base, Arbitrum, Polygon)
  • Wallet providers (MetaMask, WalletConnect, Trust Wallet)
  • DEX protocols (PancakeSwap)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. International Users

Our Services are operated from the United States. If you access our Services from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from your jurisdiction.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our Services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
